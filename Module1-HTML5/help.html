Community Event Portal - Help Page

Welcome to the Community Event Portal. This page helps users understand how to use the website.

Home

The Home section provides general information about community events and activities.

Events

The Events section displays upcoming and past community events with images and details.

Registration

To register for an event:

  1. Enter your Name.
  2. Enter your Email Address.
  3. Enter your Phone Number.
  4. Select the Event Type.
  5. Choose the Event Date.
  6. Enter your Message or Feedback.
  7. Click the Register button.

Video Section

Watch the event promotional video to learn more about upcoming activities.

Nearby Events

Click the "Find Nearby Events" button to allow location access and view your current coordinates.
